## Overview

This project showcases my high-quality photo and video editing capabilities. From intricate motion graphics and visual effects to seamless transitions and polished color grading, I specialize in transforming raw footage and images into compelling multimedia experiences.

## Core Capabilities

- **Video Editing:** Creating dynamic and engaging video content tailored for both cinematic playback and social media consumption.
- **Photo Retouching & Editing:** Enhancing images with professional color correction, detailed visual adjustments, and creative manipulation.
- **Visual Effects & Motion:** Utilizing Adobe After Effects to bring still elements to life and add professional polish to video sequences.
- **Social Media Content:** Using accessible tools like InShot for rapid, high-quality, and trendy edits optimized for platforms like Instagram and TikTok.

## Tools & Software

- Adobe After Effects
- InShot
- Adobe Premiere Pro
- Adobe Photoshop

## Approach

My editing philosophy centers on storytelling. Every cut, transition, and color grade is deliberately chosen to enhance the narrative and ensure the final product resonates powerfully with its intended audience.
